Example queries

A few questions SmartQb can answer out of the box.

Xero / QuickBooks

  • Do I have a high degree of reliance on one or a few suppliers?
  • Can I pay all my suppliers with my current cash?
  • Show me the profit and loss for last month.
  • How profitable is this month compared to the same month last year?
  • What is the average days to turn a sale to cash?

Salesforce

  • Which accounts generate the most revenue?
  • Which accounts haven’t been contacted recently?
  • Which accounts are likely to churn?
  • What activities happened on AccountX in the last 30/60/90 days?
